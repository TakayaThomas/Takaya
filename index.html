Toronto Maple Leafs

Toronto maple leafs are a professional team that is based in Toronto that play in the NGL. they compete as a member of the alantic division in the eastern conference. their club is owned by mayple leaf sports and entertainment, a company that owns a professional sports team. fir their first 14 seasons the team played home games at the mutual street arena, before moving to the maple leaf gardens in the year 1931. the maple leafs moved to their present home, scotiabank arena in the year 1999.

Toronto maple leafs was founding in 1917, operating simply as Toronto and known then as the toronto arenas. under new ownership though, the team was renamed the toronto st patricks in 1919. in 1927, the franchise was purchased by conn smythe and was renamed the maple leafs. a member of the orignial six, the club has won 13 stanley cups, second to montreal canadiens. their history includes two recognized dynasties from 1947 to 1951 and from 1962 to 1967. winning their last stanley cup in the year 1966 season, the maple leafs 57' drought between championship is the longest drought in history of NHL.

The Maple Leafs have retired the use of 13 numbers in honour of 19 players, including the first in professional sports. In addition, several individuals who hold an association with the club have been inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame. The Maple Leafs are presently affiliated with the Toronto Marlies of the American Hockey League (AHL).
